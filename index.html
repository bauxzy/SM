Powers &
Abilities

Bitten by a radioactive spider, Peter Parker gained extraordinary powers — transforming from a shy teenager into New York City's greatest defender.

🕷️
Wall-Crawling
Adheres to virtually any surface using microscopic setae on fingertips and toes.
💪
Super Strength
Can lift up to 10 tons — proportionate to the strength of a spider relative to its size.
⚡
Spider-Sense
A precognitive danger warning that fires fractions of a second before threats materialize.
🕸️
Web-Shooters
Self-designed devices that fire ultra-tensile synthetic webbing for swinging and combat.
